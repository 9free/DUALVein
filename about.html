About DUALVein

Transforming medical education through innovative technology and compassionate design

Our Story

In 2024, DUALVein was born from a simple observation: medical students train on mannequins with perfect, visible veins—but real patients are nothing like that. We witnessed countless nursing and medical students struggle during their first real IV insertions. The gap between classroom training and clinical reality was causing unnecessary pain for patients and anxiety for healthcare professionals. We knew there had to be a better way. A way to bridge the training gap without compromising on affordability or accessibility. That's when DUALVein was conceived—an AI-powered solution that makes the invisible visible.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize medical training by providing affordable, accessible, and accurate vein visualization technology that bridges the gap between classroom learning and real-world clinical practice—ultimately reducing patient discomfort and improving healthcare outcomes.

Our Vision

A future where every medical and nursing student has access to realistic training tools that prepare them for the complexities of real patients. We envision a world where failed IV attempts are rare, patient pain is minimized, and healthcare professionals feel confident from day one.

Understanding the Problem

The Training Gap

Medical and nursing students practice IV insertion on standardized mannequins with clear, shallow, and predictable veins. While this builds basic technique, it fails to prepare students for the reality of clinical practice where veins are:

  • Deep beneath the skin surface
  • Invisible to the naked eye
  • Variable across patients of different ages, skin tones, and health conditions
  • Difficult to locate in elderly, pediatric, or chronically ill patients

The Cost of Failure

The consequences of this training gap are severe. Globally, 4 billion failed IV attempts occur every year. In China alone, this results in 390 million HKD in annual compensation costs. Beyond the financial burden, patients suffer:

  • Unnecessary pain and bruising
  • Prolonged procedures and anxiety
  • Risk of complications from multiple attempts
  • Loss of trust in healthcare providers

Existing Solutions Fall Short

While vein visualization devices exist, they come with significant limitations:

  • Cost: Existing solutions cost 15,000+ HKD, making them inaccessible to most training institutions
  • Complexity: Complicated interfaces require extensive training to use effectively
  • Portability: Bulky designs limit where and how they can be used
  • Limited AI: Lack of intelligent guidance systems for students

Our Solution

DUALVein is more than just a vein visualization device—it's an intelligent training companion powered by cutting-edge AI and near-infrared technology.

AI-Powered Detection

Custom-built algorithms for precise vein segmentation that work across different skin tones, ages, and anatomies—providing real-time guidance for safer insertion.

Near-Infrared Technology

Advanced imaging reveals deep, invisible veins with clarity, giving students a realistic view of vascular anatomy they'll encounter in practice.

Accessible & Affordable

Priced 5x cheaper than competitors at under 3,000 HKD manufacturing cost, making professional-grade training accessible to institutions worldwide.

Portable Design

Compact and lightweight, DUALVein connects to any laptop—enabling training anywhere, from classrooms to clinical skills labs.

Intuitive Interface

Simple 3-step operation requires minimal training. Connect, position, and detect—ready to use in seconds.

Proven Results

75% vein detection accuracy, outperforming newly trained nurses by 30%, with 95% student approval rating.
