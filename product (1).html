The Technology

How DUALVein makes veins visible

Hemoglobin in blood absorbs near-infrared (NIR) light more strongly than surrounding tissue. DUALVein captures this contrast and projects it in real time, revealing vein location and depth invisible to the naked eye.

Our AI segmentation layer then classifies and highlights optimal insertion sites, giving trainees a guided, visual target — turning uncertainty into confidence.

  1. 1

    Connect

    Plug DUALVein into any laptop via USB — no installation required.

  2. 2

    Illuminate

    NIR light illuminates the patient's arm, revealing subcutaneous vein structure.

  3. 3

    Detect

    AI processes the image stream and segments veins in real time with 75% accuracy.

  4. 4

    Guide

    Visual overlay highlights optimal insertion sites, guiding the student to success.
